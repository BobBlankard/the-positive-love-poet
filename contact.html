Contact FAQ

What is Positive Love Poetry?

Positive Love Poetry is Dave Tebo's approach: love-oriented, concrete, visual poems that dwell on synergy, healing, and the rejuvenating joy of love. Learn more on the About page.

How quickly does Dave respond to messages?

Dave personally reads and responds to messages within 2-3 business days. Event booking inquiries with specific dates receive priority response.

Does Dave do virtual poetry readings?

Yes! Dave offers both in-person and virtual readings for weddings, corporate events, book clubs, and private gatherings. See the Media page for examples.

Can I request a custom poem?

Dave occasionally accepts commissioned poem requests for special occasions. Select "Other" in the inquiry type and describe your vision in the message.
